The Concept

Wheel-A-Way is a VR journey set in your neighbor's home. You explore room-by-room, solve environment-driven puzzles, and navigate real-life obstacles from that of a wheelchair user’s perspective to find your neighbor's missing passport in time for his flight.

Wheel-A-Way is meant to be used as both a game and a way to educate individuals on accessibility obstacles for those in wheelchairs. Our experience helps to build empathy for those with accessibility limitations and put people directly in the shoes of someone in a wheelchair.


Puzzle Based Experience Accessibility-Centered Design Exposure to Mobility Challenges VR Immersion

Do you enjoy Story-Based Games?

Wheel-A-Way is our team's take on an narrative based escape room-style game that's meant to give you both first-hand accessibility limitation exposure and allow you to have the fun of an escape room!

Do you like Puzzles?

Our experience takes you on a journey in which you must explore your neighbor's house and figure out ways to traverse this new environment to get to the passport!

Do you like New Perspectives?

Experience the world from a wheelchair user’s perspective and explore the realities of accessibility.
